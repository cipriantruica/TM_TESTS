From: farenebt@craft.camp.clarkson.edu (Droopy) Subject: AHL Leafs Back Home?  Apparently, the public workers\' strike in St John is, Newfoundland is over. The strike has, since mid-January, forced the St John is Maple Leafs hockey team to play their home games in other cities such as Toronto, Cornwall and Charlottetown. They have been playing their playoff home games in Halifax, Nova Scotia.
