Subject: roman.bmp 01/14In response to the requests for cool bitmaps I am posting one. From: pwiseman@salmon.usd.edu (Cliff)   Due to the resolution and size it is in 14 parts.  This is a uuencoded bitmap.  960X600 256 colors. The picture is a marbled gazebo on a desert with blue sky background. The size is just right for centered wallpaper on a 1024x768 display because it leaves a border at the bottom just big enough for icons to fit in without being on top of the picture. Reguarding image quality and resolution - I have not seen much better.  For those of you who have not worked with pieced image files, here is how to put it back together. \t1. save the 14 parts to 14 individual files \t2. use a text editor to remove the header and footer \t   in each file. \t3. concatinate all the parts into a single file \t4. uudecode the file There are tools available to do this without the hassle such as uuconvert (UNIX) and UUcoder (Windows).
