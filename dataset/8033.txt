From: maven@mavenry.altcit.eskimo.com (Norman Hamer) Subject: Good Reasons to Wave at each other     One of those  morning, just getting the coffee in me  thoughts:    Waving at other bikers makes more sense than just  Hey, how has it going,  nice to meet you on the road, have a good ride     1) If you are watching for other bikes to wave to, it means your attention  is on the road, where it should be, and you are more likely to see cages.    2) It keeps you in the habit of watching really carefully for bikes when  you are IN a cage. This is a Good Thing.
