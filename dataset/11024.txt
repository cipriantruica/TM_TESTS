From: boucher@csl.sri.com (Peter K. Boucher) Subject: Pseudo-Random Character Generators (large state)  Here are four pseudo-random character generators, based on irreducible trinomials.  Each contains 16 separate trinomials, one of which is selected on initialization (there are 64 distinct trinomials between the 4 PRCGs).  The PRCGs are initialized with a 32-bit seed, and a 4-bit trinomial- selector.  I would like to get comments on these by anyone who is interested enough to look them over.  Please email, because our news is on the fritz (Note that this was posted via email).  Peter K.
